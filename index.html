Explore Madhya Pradesh

Discover the Heart of Incredible India with ancient temples, lush forests, wildlife, waterfalls, and breathtaking landscapes.

Popular Destinations

Amarkantak

Visit the majestic Kapildhara Waterfall, where the sacred Narmada River cascades over rocky cliffs amidst the lush forests of the Maikal Hills.

Pachmarhi

Pachmarhi, the Queen of Satpura, is Madhya Pradesh's only hill station. Known for its lush forests, waterfalls, caves, viewpoints, and pleasant climate, it is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Khajuraho

A UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its magnificent temples and intricate sculptures.
